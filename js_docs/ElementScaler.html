Class: ElementScaler

ElementScaler

the ElementScaler is meant to ensure that the application always fits within the browser window vertically. Most of the scaling happens in media queries in the style sheet!!!

Constructor

new ElementScaler()

ElementScaler constructor. References elements, and sets a timer that rescales the elements after the window's size changed. That scaling also happens once initially.
